--- layout: default title: About John ---

John Holbrook is a high school student, robotics enthusiast, and amateur computer scientist based concurrently in Huntington, WV, USA and Orleans, MA, USA.

John is a senior at Huntington High School in Huntington, where he is a founding member of the school's VEX Robotics Competition and Science Olympiad teams. John's other endeavours in robotics include mentoring teams participating in the FIRST LEGO League and his role as half of the Hello World! YouTube channel.

John also enjoys playing the viola and has been a member of the Tri-State Youth Orchestra since 2012.

John's other areas of interest include engineering, physics, 3D printing, computer science, mathematics, and plastering his logo on things.

In the summer months, John is employed by the Orleans Yacht Club Junior Sailing Program, where he helps to instruct children aged 8-18 in sailing. John is also a member of the program's 420 race team.

John never thought he would be so pompous as to write a third-person biography of himself, but here he is.
