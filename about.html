--- layout: default title: About John ---

John Holbrook is a student, robotics enthusiast, and amateur computer scientist based concurrently in Athens, OH, Huntington, WV, and Orleans, MA, USA.

John is a student ot Ohio University in Athens, where he is pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.

John graduated from Huntington High School in Huntington, where he was a founding member of the school's VEX Robotics Competition and Science Olympiad teams. John's other endeavours in robotics include mentoring teams participating in the FIRST LEGO League and his role as half of the Hello World! YouTube channel.

John also enjoys playing the viola and was been a member of the Tri-State Youth Orchestra from 2012 to 2016.

John's other areas of interest include engineering, physics, 3D printing, mathematics, and plastering his logo on things.

In the summer months, John is employed by the Orleans Yacht Club Junior Sailing Program, where he helps to instruct children aged 8-18 in sailing, with a particular interest in advanced boat-handling techniques, race strategy, and applying principles of physics to sailing. From 2013 to 2016, John was also a member of the program's 420 race team.

John never thought he would be so pompous as to write a third-person biography of himself, but here he is.
